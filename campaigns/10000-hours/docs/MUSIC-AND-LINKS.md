# Music and External-Link Policy

The **10,000 Hours of BMX Inspiration Mixed Tape** is a documentary presentation of a Lititz BMX campaign.

- No song recordings are included.
- No music videos are included.
- External video links are preserved because they formed part of the published campaign pages.
- Availability, titles, thumbnails and ownership of external videos may change outside the control of Lititz BMX.
- Artist and song labels are recorded only when stated on the supplied page or visible in the supplied page capture.
- The campaign wording, BMX imagery and archival arrangement remain separate from the externally hosted music.
