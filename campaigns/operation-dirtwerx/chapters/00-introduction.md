## Archival Summary

The introduction identifies the bicycle as Harry Leary's DIRTWERX and frames the campaign as a preservation project centered on return, documentation, and understanding. It credits contributions from the Leary family and expressly states that the acquisition story will remain offline.

## Original Campaign-Page Text

```text
#OperationDIRTWERX - The Story

Bringing Harry’s Bike Home

This story was developed with contributions from the Leary family.

This was Harry Leary’s bike.

At some point, it left his hands — passed down, moved on, nearly lost to time.

But unlike so many pieces of BMX history…
it found its way back.

Then the work began.

Getting the bike is a story that will stay "offline" - but it is a doozy that includes a Team USA rider.
```
