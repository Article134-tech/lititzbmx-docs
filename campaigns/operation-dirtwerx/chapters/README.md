## Governing Sequence Rule

The numbered narrative sequence and the dated source chronology answer different questions:

- **Narrative sequence** preserves how the campaign page presents the story.
- **Source chronology** preserves when the surviving source records were published.

Neither sequence is silently rewritten to force agreement with the other.
