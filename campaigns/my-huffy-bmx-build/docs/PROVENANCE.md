# Provenance

This archive was compiled from the ten public Google Sites chapter pages, user-supplied chapter text, contemporaneous Facebook captures, completed-bike photographs, Sugar Cayne publication captures, Coleman Bike Park photographs, and the related YouTube Short record.

## Source separation

1. Original image files are preserved byte-for-byte under `source/`.
2. Original chapter language supplied by Kyle A. Huffman is presented in the chapter book layer.
3. Public-page captures preserve the Google Sites layout and published title forms.
4. Later archival notes identify evidence boundaries without replacing the source.
5. SHA-256 hashes document file identity.

## Key provenance statements

- Harry Leary’s seat and seat post entered the build through Linda Leary Taylor.
- DIRTWERX-related grip hardware moved forward from the documented #OperationDIRTWERX project.
- The Harry-linked vintage chain was considered but not installed.
- The Huffy itself was not owned or ridden by Harry Leary.
- The Sugar Cayne article is an external publication record.
- The Coleman Bike Park images document continuing use and a same-visit media-production context; visible social-publication dates do not automatically equal filming dates.
