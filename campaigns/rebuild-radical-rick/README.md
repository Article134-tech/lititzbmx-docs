# Rebuild Radical Rick

**Status:** Archived  
**Campaign period:** March 12–28, 2025  
**Campaign:** #RebuildRadicalRick  
**Produced by:** Lititz BMX

---

## Campaign Overview

In March 2025, Lititz BMX launched a multi-part social-media campaign documenting the reconstruction of a 40th Anniversary Radical Rick figure.

What began as a collection of unidentified figure components became a serialized story connecting BMX history, comic culture, preservation, community participation, and the work of Radical Rick creator Damian Fulton.

The campaign followed the figure piece by piece, explored the history surrounding Radical Rick, incorporated related interviews and archival material, and concluded with a completed figure, a community giveaway, and a final retrospective video.

---

## Campaign Goals

The campaign was created to:

- document the reconstruction of the 40th Anniversary Radical Rick figure;
- introduce audiences to Radical Rick and BMX comic history;
- connect physical artifacts with interviews, publications, and historical sources;
- encourage participation through serialized reveals and questions;
- promote the preservation of BMX history through storytelling;
- direct audiences to related Lititz BMX interviews and educational material;
- preserve the completed campaign as a permanent archival record.

---

## Campaign Scope

The campaign contains **19 officially numbered episode positions**.

- **18 distinct episodes have been verified**
- **Episode 15 remains unverified and is preserved as “The Lost Episode”**
- The official numbering does not always follow publication-date order
- Both the numbered sequence and chronological sequence are preserved in the campaign ledger

No missing material has been reconstructed through assumption.
