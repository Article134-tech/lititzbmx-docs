## Episode Summary

Episode 16 presented a final one-day teaser before the conclusion of the #RebuildRadicalRick campaign.

The accompanying image showed the completed figure’s head positioned in front of *Radical Rick: The Complete Episodes*, while the brief post announced that everything would be revealed the following day.

Although Episode 14 had already documented completion of the physical reconstruction, Episode 16 shifted the campaign toward its final reveal, giveaway result, and retrospective video.

## Archival Context

Episode 16 served as the campaign’s final teaser before the March 28 finale.

The physical reconstruction had already been declared complete in Episode 14. Episode 18, published on March 26, continued the countdown and asked audiences what they had learned and enjoyed during the campaign.

Published on March 27, Episode 16 reduced the message to a simple announcement: the full result would be revealed the following day.

The image connected the completed head component with *Radical Rick: The Complete Episodes*, one of the central historical and giveaway-related objects featured throughout the campaign.

The brevity of the post distinguished it from the more detailed historical and educational episodes. Its purpose was anticipation rather than additional explanation.
