# The Radical Rick Collection

## A Digital Graphic-Novel Exhibition

<p align="center"><a href="volumes/volume-1/"><img src="source/hero.jpg" alt="Physical Lititz BMX Radical Rick display with artwork, publications, figures and a mystery package." width="1000"></a></p>

> **From printed page to preserved artifact.** The collection is arranged in volumes and issues, but every visual panel is an authentic source image. The graphic-novel structure is an access framework—not newly created art and not a claim that the objects were historically published in this order.

The Radical Rick Collection brings together original Damian Fulton artwork, publications, custom figures, limited editions, a Harry Leary racing award, the complete #RebuildRadicalRick campaign and continuing media records. The series architecture allows the digital collection to be published now and to grow later without pretending that all physical holdings have already been inventoried.

## Curatorial principle

A graphic novel does not have to end because one issue is complete. Volume One preserves the documented collection available now; future discoveries can correct a panel, expand the registry or open a new volume. The physical collection may continue to change, but the source images, provenance, relationships and state of knowledge represented here remain preserved.
