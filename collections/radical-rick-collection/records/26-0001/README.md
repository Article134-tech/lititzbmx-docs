## The story

“Dirty Fest 3” is an original watercolor painting by Radical Rick creator Damian Fulton, created for the Dirty Fest vintage BMX event. The artwork celebrates old-school BMX culture and features numerous legendary riders alongside the Radical Rick character. As a one-of-one original piece of BMX artwork, the painting represents the influence of Fulton’s illustrations on the visual culture of BMX history.
