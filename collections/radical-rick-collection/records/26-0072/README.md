## The story

Original hand-painted mini portrait of Radical Rick created by artist Damian Fulton for the 2024 NoWear BMX Compound fundraiser. Produced to commemorate the 45th anniversary of the character, each portrait is unique and part of a limited run of 45 pieces worldwide. The artwork is hand-signed by Fulton and features the embossed “Certified Radical” seal verifying authenticity.

## Inside the panel

- Hand-painted mini portrait.
- Produced for the 2024 NoWear BMX Compound fundraiser according to the inventory.
- Signed by Damian Fulton and marked with the Certified Radical seal according to the inventory.

## How it entered the collection

Source recorded as Damian Fulton in the Lititz BMX inventory.

## What remains qualified

The original source title spells the organization “Nowhere BMX”; the inventory description uses “NoWear BMX Compound.” Both forms are preserved as a known wording discrepancy.
