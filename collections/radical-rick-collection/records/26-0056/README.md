## The story

One of only eight full-color press proofs produced for the cover of Radical Rick: The Complete Episodes. This example, numbered 2 of 8, was used by creator Damian Fulton to verify color accuracy and final finish prior to publication. Printed on 12” x 18” heavy stock, it features a hand-crimped “Certified Radical” gold foil seal and is hand-signed and numbered by Fulton.

## Inside the panel
