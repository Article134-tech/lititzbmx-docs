## The story

A serialized 2025 Lititz BMX campaign documenting the reconstruction of a 40th Anniversary Radical Rick figure. The campaign archive preserves 19 officially numbered episode positions, 18 verified episodes and one unresolved lost episode without reconstructing missing evidence.

## Inside the panel

- Campaign period: March 12–28, 2025.
- Nineteen official episode positions.
- Eighteen verified episodes.
- Episode 15 preserved as The Lost Episode.

## How it entered the collection

The complete campaign archive is already preserved in the repository under campaigns/rebuild-radical-rick/.

## What remains qualified

The collection does not duplicate the episode archive. It presents the campaign as a connected storyline and directs readers to the canonical campaign record.
