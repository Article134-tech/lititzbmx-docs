## The story

A commemorative Radical Rick bobblehead produced for the character’s 45th anniversary through a collaboration between Damian Fulton and Supercross BMX founder Bill Ryan. Finished in gold-plated style resin, only 45 units were produced worldwide. Each example is hand-signed and numbered by Fulton, representing a modern collectible celebrating the enduring influence of Radical Rick within BMX culture.

## Inside the panel
