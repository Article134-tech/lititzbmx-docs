## The story

Limited edition print illustrated by Radical Rick creator Damian Fulton celebrating two of the most recognizable icons of the 1980s BMX era. The artwork unites Radical Rick with Bill Allen, internationally known for portraying Cru Jones in the 1986 BMX film RAD. Hand-signed by both Fulton and Allen, this piece is numbered 57 of 100 and bridges BMX comic culture with the cinematic legacy of Helltrack.

## Inside the panel
