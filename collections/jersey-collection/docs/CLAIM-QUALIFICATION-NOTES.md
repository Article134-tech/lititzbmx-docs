# Curatorial and claim-qualification notes

## Current holdings versus documentary records

The 22 accessioned entries are presented as the current Digital Jersey Wall. The five “continued journey” entries are visibly separated and are not represented as current Lititz BMX holdings.

## Source hierarchy

1. Curator-supplied current Jersey Collection titles and provenance labels.
2. Preserved Google Sites collection captures.
3. Current Lititz BMX inventory CSV descriptions.
4. Preserved social-media posts and comments as publication and research context.

Comments and community recollections remain attributed. They are not converted into independently verified institutional findings without separate support.

## Wear and ownership wording

“Worn,” “race-worn,” “owned,” “signed,” “gifted,” and similar terms are used only where present in the current inventory, public collection, or curator-supplied record. Generic jerseys are not assigned to a specific wearer without evidence.

## Derivative wall cards

The 27 normalized wall-card images are later archival access derivatives cropped from the three preserved Google Sites wall captures. The complete source captures are retained byte-for-byte in `source/google-sites/`.
