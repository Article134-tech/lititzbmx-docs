# Known metadata discrepancies

The archive preserves visible conflicts rather than silently rewriting them.

## 26.0035

The live Jersey Collection and curator-supplied list identify **26.0035 as a JAG Jersey purchased from the BMX Museum**. The current inventory CSV identifies **26.0035 as a “JAG Kit with Oakley Factory Pilot Decal,” category Gear, source Online**. Version 1.0.0 follows the current public Jersey Collection for the Digital Jersey Wall and flags the inventory conflict for later reconciliation.

## 26.0080

Some inventory exports abbreviate the Fall Risk Racing “Leary” jersey accession as **26.008**. The live collection and curator-supplied list use **26.0080**, which is the stable identifier used here.

- The accessioned **26.0063 Harry Leary DIRTWERX Jersey** is distinct from the DIRTWERX jersey gifted to Justin Dumas.
