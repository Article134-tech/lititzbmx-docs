# Provenance and stewardship

The Jersey Collection records several forms of provenance:

- material recovered through the Leary Locker;
- gifts from Greg Mathias and John Stancliff;
- material purchased through the BMX Museum or online;
- jerseys returned or gifted to teammates and friends;
- a jersey directed into a charitable auction.

This architecture intentionally separates **current custody** from **historical association**. A transferred jersey remains searchable and citable as part of the collection’s documentary history, but its former presence is not used to imply current ownership.

The paired Fall Risk Racing jerseys and the 2017 Team USA return demonstrate reciprocal preservation: an artifact can leave the archive while generating new relationships and new material for preservation.
