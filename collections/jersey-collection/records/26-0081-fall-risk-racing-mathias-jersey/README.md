## Why this jersey matters

This Fall Risk Racing “Mathias” jersey was gifted by BMX racer Greg Mathias to Lititz BMX in gratitude after the archive presented him with the 2017 Team USA “Learry” jersey discovered within the Leary Locker collection. The jersey reflects the camaraderie within the BMX community and the collaborative effort to preserve and share the sport’s history.

## Additional context

Fall Risk Racing team jersey worn by Greg Mathias and donated to the Lititz BMX Archive as part of the same exchange surrounding the recovery of the Team USA “Leary” jersey from the Leary Locker collection. The artifact highlights the close-knit nature of the BMX community and the cooperation between riders and historians in safeguarding BMX heritage.
