## Why this jersey matters

An authentic, race-worn Factory Team Jersey produced by DIRTWERX for BMX legend Harry Leary. This jersey features the classic heavy-duty ventilation and padded elbows characteristic of the Pro-Circuit era. As a companion piece to the DIRTWERX helmet and Leary's signature frames, this garment is a primary artifact of 80s/90s racing 'style' and functional design.

## Additional context

This factory team jersey produced by DIRTWERX for Harry Leary. The garment features heavy ventilation panels and reinforced elbow padding typical of professional BMX racewear in the late 1980s and early 1990s. As a companion piece to Leary’s helmet and race bikes, the jersey represents the functional design and visual style of the professional BMX circuit during the sport’s “Pro-Class” era.
