## Why this jersey matters

This BIOLAB “Leary 4” jersey produced by Zeronine Apparel was worn by BMX legend Harry Leary during his later racing career. The jersey features Leary’s name and the number 4, reflecting his race number while competing with BIOLAB Sciences sponsorship. This artifact pairs with item 26.0076 in the Lititz BMX Archive and is documented in photographs of Leary wearing the jersey during competition and promotional appearances.

## Additional context

Racing jersey produced by Zeronine Apparel for Harry Leary during his later professional racing years under BIOLAB Sciences sponsorship. The jersey features Leary’s name and race number “4” as part of the coordinated team kit. This artifact pairs with item 26.0076 in the Lititz BMX Archive and is documented in period photographs of Leary wearing the jersey during competition and promotional appearances.
