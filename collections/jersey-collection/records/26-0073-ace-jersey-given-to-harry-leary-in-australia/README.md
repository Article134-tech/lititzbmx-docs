## Why this jersey matters

A rare piece of international BMX history: an original ACE (Australian Cycling Enterprises) jersey presented to Harry Leary during his competitive tours in Australia. This garment signifies the global expansion of the sport and the cross-continental influence of U.S. Factory Pros. A vital 'Survivor' piece that documents Harry's status as a global ambassador for the sport.

## Additional context

Original ACE (Australian Cycling Enterprises) jersey presented to Harry Leary during one of his competitive tours in Australia. The garment reflects the growing international reach of BMX during the 1980s and the role of American professional riders in promoting the sport abroad. As a presentation piece tied to Leary’s travels, it documents BMX’s early global expansion.
