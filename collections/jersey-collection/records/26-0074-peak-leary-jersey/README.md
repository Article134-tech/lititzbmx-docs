## Why this jersey matters

A rare co-branded artifact from the peak of Harry Leary's career: the Peak Performance x BIOLAB factory jersey. This piece documents a pivotal era where BMX moved into the realm of high-performance athletics, with BIOLAB serving as the sport's early leader in rider conditioning and sports science. Featuring the iconic neon-heavy sublimation of Peak and the distinctive BIOLAB 'laboratory' branding.

## Additional context

Co-branded Peak Performance and BIOLAB factory team jersey associated with Harry Leary during the height of his professional career. The design features the vibrant neon sublimation style characteristic of late-1980s performance racewear along with BIOLAB branding, a company known for promoting rider conditioning and sports science within BMX. The jersey reflects a period when BMX increasingly embraced high-performance athletic training.
