## Why this jersey matters

This Fall Risk Racing “Leary” jersey was gifted by BMX racer Greg Mathias in gratitude to Lititz BMX after the archive presented him with the 2017 Team USA “Learry” jersey discovered within the Leary Locker collection. The exchange reflects the strong sense of community and mutual respect within BMX, where riders and historians often work together to preserve and share the sport’s history.

## Additional context

Fall Risk Racing team jersey bearing Harry Leary’s name, gifted by BMX racer Greg Mathias to the Lititz BMX Archive. The jersey was presented in gratitude after the archive returned a 2017 Team USA “Leary” jersey discovered within the Leary Locker collection. The exchange reflects the collaborative spirit within BMX culture and the shared effort to preserve the sport’s history.
