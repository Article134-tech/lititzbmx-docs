## Why this jersey matters

This BIOLAB “Leary 25” jersey was worn by BMX pioneer Harry Leary during his later racing years. Sponsored by BIOLAB, the jersey reflects Leary’s continued involvement in competitive BMX decades after helping define the sport’s early racing era.

## Additional context

Racing jersey worn by BMX pioneer Harry Leary during his later competitive years while sponsored by BIOLAB Sciences. Featuring Leary’s name and race number “25,” the jersey reflects his continued presence in BMX competition decades after helping shape the sport’s early professional racing era.
