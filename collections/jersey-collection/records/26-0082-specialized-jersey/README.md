## Why this jersey matters

This Specialized racing jersey represents the long-standing involvement of Specialized Bicycle Components in BMX and cycling culture. Jerseys like this were worn by riders and team members during races and promotional events, reflecting the brand identity and sponsorship presence that became a central part of competitive BMX.
