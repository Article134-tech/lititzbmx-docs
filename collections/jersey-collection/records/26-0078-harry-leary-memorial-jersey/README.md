## Why this jersey matters

This Harry Leary memorial jersey was produced for a gathering held to celebrate the life and legacy of BMX pioneer Harry Leary at Dirtyfest 3. The jerseys were created by fellow BMX racer Greg Mathias for the event, which brought together riders and friends from across the BMX community to honor Leary’s lasting impact on the sport.

## Additional context

Commemorative jersey produced for the gathering held during Dirtyfest 3 to celebrate the life and legacy of BMX pioneer Harry Leary. Designed and produced by fellow racer Greg Mathias, the jerseys were worn by friends and riders who attended the event honoring Leary’s contributions to BMX culture and racing history.
