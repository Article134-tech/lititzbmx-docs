# Known Gaps

Harry’s Room is a complete integration of the source package supplied for this release, but it is not presented as a final inventory of every Harry Leary artifact held by Lititz BMX.

## Direct image gap

- **26.0076 — BIOLAB “Harry” Zeronine Pants:** a direct photograph was not supplied. The record uses a clearly labeled context image showing the matching jersey and Zeronine source post.

## Source-capture gap

- A full-page capture of original source B was not supplied. Its URL, supplied artifact list and individual images are preserved in the source inventory and crosswalk.

## Provenance depth

- Detailed acquisition histories were not supplied for every artifact. Records distinguish known provenance from collection attribution rather than assuming that every object followed the same acquisition path.

## Authentication depth

- Signatures, visual identifications and production-status claims are preserved with qualifications where independent authentication or production records were not supplied.

## Future growth

New artifacts should be added to the appropriate room zone and the machine-readable register. They should not be introduced as “Page 3” or another source-page continuation.
