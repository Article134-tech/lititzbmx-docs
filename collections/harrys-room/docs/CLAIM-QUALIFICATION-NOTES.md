# Claim-Qualification Notes

Harry’s Room separates what is visible on an object from what is stated in a collection caption or research post.

## Claim-status vocabulary

- **inscription-supported:** key wording is visible on the physical artifact.
- **source-supported:** the image and supplied collection description support the normalized record without a major unresolved conflict.
- **document-supported:** a letter, credential or other document directly supports the record.
- **mixed-source:** part of the record is visible and part comes from the supplied caption.
- **collection-attributed:** the collection identifies the object, rider, event or relationship; independent authentication was not supplied.
- **visual-comparison-attribution:** identification is based on comparison with footage or photographs.
- **attributed-unresolved:** the source preserves active questions, approximate dates or recollections that should not be converted into verified findings.

## Signatures

The Jeremy McGrath signatures on 26.0038 and the signatures associated with 26.0051 are preserved as collection attributions. This release does not claim independent signature authentication.

## Source images

Social-post screenshots and collection-page captures are evidence of publication and curatorial context. Visible engagement counts are capture-time interface data and are not treated as permanent collection facts.

## No invented completion

Where the source is incomplete, Harry’s Room records the gap. It does not invent missing dates, production counts, provenance transfers, event outcomes or direct images.
