## Accessible text version

**Welcome to the Lititz BMX Archive Scavenger Hunt.**

This beginner-level experience is designed to help you explore the site, discover key artifacts, and begin connecting with the history of BMX. As you navigate through collections, individual items, and embedded media, you’ll uncover stories that shaped the sport—from its early days to the culture it represents today.

Take your time, follow your curiosity, and enjoy the ride.

> **“Riders Ready…”**

### The Gate Drop - Enter the Archive

Navigate to LititzBMX.com. You should land on the Welcome page - the starting gate of the archive.

### First Straight - Explore the Archive Inventory

Locate the main inventory.

Review how the archive is organized. What patterns do you notice in how items are cataloged?

### First Berm - Navigate Through the Artifacts

Select an artifact page and explore it.

What drew your attention to this artifact?

What media helped you understand the artifact?

What connections stand out to you?

### Second Straight - Follow a Rider’s Story

Find a rider profile and learn about their background.

What makes their story significant to BMX history?

### Second Berm - Make a Connection

Identify another artifact or page connected to that rider.

How are the two linked?

### Third Straight - Explore a Collection

Navigate to a collection page.

What theme or story connects the items within it?

### Third Berm - Discover the Bigger Picture

Find a campaign, learning resource, or story page.

What message or idea is being shared?

### One more push...

### Finish Line - Reflect

What is one artifact, story, or connection that stood out to you?

Why does it matter?
