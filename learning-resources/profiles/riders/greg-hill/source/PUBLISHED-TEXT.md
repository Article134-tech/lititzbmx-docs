# Published source transcription — Greg Hill

> Preserved from the supplied Lititz BMX profile text. This transcription records publication wording and does not convert every statement into an independently verified fact.

Rider Profile: Gregory Alan Hill - Greg Hill

Current Occupation: Owner/Operator of Epoxy Surface Professionals (ESP) in the Los Angeles area

D.O.B.: 27 October, 1963

Began Racing: 1974 at Escape County Track in Trabuco, CA

First Race Bike: Schwinn “Apple Crate”

First National Win: National Bike Association (NBA) Winter Nationals in Scottsdale, Az - April 17th, 1979 (Three years to the day before Kyle, Lititz BMX founder, was born)

Turned Professional: Age 14 - 28 March, 1978

Retired as a Pro (Senior AA Pro): Age 35 - 1998

Sampling of Major Wins:

- 1975/76 Local No.1 12 Expert
- 1977 National No.1 14 Expert
- 1978 National No.1 15 Expert
- 1979 NBA National # 1 Champion National Bicycle Association (NBA)
- 1980 Race of Champions Pro winner
- 1981 Pro Money and Senior Open Grandnational Champion
- 1982 Jag Pro World Champion (ABA sanctioned)
- 1983 ESPN Pro Spectacular Series No. 1 Champion (NBL sanctioned)
- 1985 Pro Cruiser Grandnational Champion
- 1985 National No.1 Pro & Pro Cruiser National No.1
- 1986 National No.1 Pro Cruiser
- 1988 National No.1 Pro
- 1996 Veteran Pro World Cup Champion

Started GHP in the Summer of 1983

Last closed GHP in 2017

Four-time BMX Action Number One Racer Award (NORA) Cup winner

Wife: Liz Torres-Hill - A BMX Legend herself who is in The BMX HOF’s Great Eights for 2025 - Current BMX HOF “The Great 8’s” Nominee

Married: 7 January 1994
