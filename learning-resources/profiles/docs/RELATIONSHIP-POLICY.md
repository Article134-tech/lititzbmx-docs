# Relationship Policy

## Independent records remain independent

Rider profiles, brand profiles, track-index entries, artifacts, collections, campaigns and media dossiers are separate catalog objects. A person, publication, brand or place appearing in another record may justify a contextual link, but it does not merge the records or establish common ownership, provenance, custody or evidentiary weight.

## Allowed relationship labels

- **visual/content appearance** — a person, object, logo or place is visibly present in a source image;
- **source citation** — one record cites another publication or website;
- **contextual archive link** — another Lititz BMX record may help a reader continue research;
- **explicit documented association** — the supplied source directly states the relationship.

## Prohibited shortcuts

The archive does not infer a relationship solely from proximity, shared page placement, a repeated name, a matching image, geography, chronology or outside knowledge. Uncertain relationships remain uncertain.
