# Known Gaps and Source Limitations — v1.0.0

## Track capture completeness

All ten Track Profiles source pages are complete. Twenty source captures—top and bottom for every page—are preserved, and all 150 published visual labels are transcribed in page order.

The track records remain visual-index records. They document that each named track or location appeared in the published Lititz BMX index with the accompanying image; they do not independently verify dates, ownership, operators, sanctioning bodies, rider participation, event history or present operating status.

## Mike Poulson capture format

The supplied Mike Poulson source is a public profile/social capture rather than a polished full Google Sites page capture. The full supplied text is preserved, and the image role is identified accurately.

## Source-bound discrepancies

Examples preserved in the individual records include Kalsow/Kaslow spelling, the Mike Miranda age/date conflict, Tommy Brackens’ retirement/sponsor sequence, approximate height formatting and AI-generated Copilot passages. These are retained as evidence of the published source and qualified rather than silently corrected.
