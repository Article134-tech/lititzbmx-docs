# Archival Notes

This release creates one front door and three independent wings:

1. Rider Profiles — ten source-bound learning profiles;
2. Brand Profiles — four independent brand/publication profiles;
3. Track Profiles — a complete ten-page visual atlas preserving 150 labels, page order, source URLs and 20 page captures.

All supplied images are copied byte-for-byte under stable Windows-safe paths. Exact transcriptions are separated from visitor-facing summaries. Machine-readable registers support discovery without replacing the source images or original wording.

Catalog identity and contextual appearance remain distinct. A person appearing on a magazine cover, for example, creates a documented visual/content relationship but does not merge the independent person and publication records or establish shared provenance.
