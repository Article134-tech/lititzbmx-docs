# Source Qualification

These GitHub records preserve supplied Lititz BMX publication pages and text. Their job is to preserve what was published, identify where claims came from and keep uncertainty visible.

- Direct quotations retain attribution.
- Current occupations, residences, nominations and “currently” statements are time-bound to the source.
- Copilot-labeled text remains explicitly AI-supplied and is not treated as independent verification.
- Reported, disputed, approximate and uncertain claims retain those qualifiers.
- Spelling, date and sequence discrepancies are documented rather than silently corrected.
- Track pages are visual index records only; no absent history is generated.
