The supplied retrospective does not identify the 1980 NORA Cup No. 1 Team. That answer was verified against a published BMX Action historical listing:

## Known verification notes

- **PRO-BABLY** is preserved from the quiz; the article prints **probably**.
- **Avalance** is preserved from the quiz; the article prints **Avalanche**.
- Question 5 is **False**; the supplied article identifies Stu Thomsen as the 1980 NORA Cup winner.
- The NORA Cup team answer **Mongoose** comes from the supplemental published BMX Action listing, not from the supplied retrospective.
- **Juicy Jaws** is the verified nickname associated with Jason Jenson’s size 4½ footprints.
