## Critical verification findings

### Question 1 preserves a printed wordplay

The quiz prints **“PRO-BABLY.”** The article prints **“probably.”** The answer remains Jason Jenson, and the quiz wording is preserved rather than silently normalized.

### Question 4 contains a published misspelling

The quiz prints **“THE AVALANCE.”** The article caption prints **“The Avalanche.”**

The archival transcription preserves **Avalance** as published in the quiz, while the answer key and verification findings identify the article-supported form **Avalanche**.

### Question 5 is false

The supplied 1980 retrospective says that Stu Thomsen won his first NORA Cup. A separate published BMX Action historical listing identifies **Stu Thomsen** as the 1980 NORA Cup rider.

### The team-award answer required supplemental verification

The supplied 1980 retrospective spread does not print the NORA Cup No. 1 Team winner. A later published BMX Action historical listing records:

- 1979 — Shimano
- **1980 — Mongoose**
- 1981 — Red Line
- 1982 — Red Line

The answer **Mongoose** is therefore retained with its supplemental source clearly identified.
