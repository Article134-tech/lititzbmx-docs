**Archive package version:** 1.0  
**Prepared:** July 19, 2026  
**Series status:** Complete archived sequence through 1986  
**Governing standard:** The preservation model established for **#RebuildRadicalRick**

## Protocol acknowledgement

**I will not change shit about a process without consulting Kyle first.**

The approved workflow remains locked:

1. receive one complete quiz entry;
2. preserve every supplied image and URL;
3. transcribe the quiz and supporting source;
4. build and verify the archive record immediately;
5. identify unresolved answers, discrepancies, misspellings, and source gaps without silently correcting them; and
6. complete the deliverable before advancing to the next quiz.
