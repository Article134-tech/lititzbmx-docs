This GitHub-ready package preserves the 1984 entry in the Lititz BMX history-quiz learning series under the same locked archival workflow used for the preceding Lititz BMX GitHub preservation projects:

## Critical verification findings

### Shaun Texas / Shawn Texas

The quiz prints **Shaun Texas**. The article prints **Shawn Texas**. Both are preserved exactly where they occur, and the discrepancy is prominently documented.

### Tommy Brackens statement is false

The supplied article says Tommy Brackens ended 1984 **off Torker and on GT**. The quiz statement saying he ended with Tommy Brackens Products is therefore false.

### Greg Hill/GHP statement is true

The article says Hill won his second consecutive NORA Cup and that it was his first one with GHP.
