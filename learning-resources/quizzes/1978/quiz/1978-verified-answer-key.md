## Critical verification findings

### Published surname error in Question 2

The quiz reads **“JEFF RUIMNER.”** The supporting article reads **“Jeff Ruminer.”**

Archival treatment:

- preserve **RUIMNER** in the original quiz transcription;
- use **Jeff Ruminer** when identifying the individual from the source article;
- flag the discrepancy prominently;
- do not silently rewrite the quiz image or its published text.

### Published distractor spelling

Extra-credit option D reads **“PITTSBURG, PA.”** The Pennsylvania city is normally spelled **Pittsburgh**. Because this is a published distractor rather than the verified answer, it is preserved exactly and documented without alteration.
