## Published-text verification notes

1. The quiz spells the name **RENNY ROKER**. The supplied supporting article also spells the name **Renny Roker**.
2. The known series-level erroneous form **Renny Rocker** is not present in the supplied 1979 quiz or article.
3. Question 2 writes **“COCA COLA COWBOY”** without the hyphenation used in the article’s **“Coca-Cola Cowboy.”**
4. Question 4 uses the phrase **“JAG WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP SERIES.”** The article describes the first Jag World Championships and the short tradition that followed, rather than printing that exact phrase.
