## Critical verification findings

### Renny Roker name check

The current 1979 quiz and supporting article both use **Renny Roker**.

The separately known erroneous form **Renny Rocker** is not present in the supplied 1979 materials. This package therefore preserves **Renny Roker** and explicitly records that the spelling was checked rather than silently assumed.

### Question 2 is contextually stated

The article does not print a standalone sentence reading “Toby Henderson was the Coca-Cola Cowboy.” It first names **Toby Henderson** and **Jeff Bottema**, then refers to the pair as **“the ‘Coca-Cola Cowboy’ and Bottema.”** The answer is therefore supported by contextual attribution.

### Question 3 is answered by exclusion

The source identifies three answer choices as DG riders—Sal Zuener, Toby Henderson, and Jeff Bottema. **Harry Leary** is the only listed choice not identified with DG in the supplied article.

### Question 4 wording is broader than the article

The quiz calls it the **Jag World Championship Series**. The source describes the **first ever Jag World Championships** and says it became a short tradition. The answer remains Renny Roker, but the wording difference is preserved and documented.

### Source scope

All five main questions and the extra-credit question are supported by the supplied article spread. No external answer key was invented.
