## 1983

### 1
**A TWO PAGE ARTICLE WAS WRITTEN IN BMXA CALLING A TRICK THE __________ BUT IT BECAME “THE LEARY.”**

- B. MIKE POULSON

- A. ERIC RUPE
- B. GREG GRUBBS
- C. GARY ELLIS
- D. ANTHONY SEWELL

**T/F - GREG HILL WON HIS FIRST NORA CUP WHILE RIDING FOR HUTCH, THEN LAUNCHED GREG HILL PRODUCTS (GHP) IN OCTOBER ’83, AND BECAME A DAD IN DECEMBER.**

### 5

## Published-text verification notes

1. **BMXA**, **NORA**, **GHP**, and the rider names are retained as published.

3. Question 4 is **False** because the supplied source states that Greg Hill was riding for **GT**, not Hutch.
