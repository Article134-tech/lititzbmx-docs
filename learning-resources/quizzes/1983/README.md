## Critical verification findings

### Question 4 is false for a specific reason

Greg Hill’s first NORA Cup, the GHP launch, and his becoming a father are all described in the article. The false element is the claim that he was riding for Hutch; the source says he was riding for GT.

### The trick-name controversy is preserved

The source records Judge, Leary, Medrano, and Utterback as competing names. BMXA’s two-page instructional spread called it a Judge, while the article ultimately credits Harry Leary through earlier photographic evidence.

### ESPN-series money is scoped correctly

The first six races offered $5,000 each. The seventh and final race offered $15,000.
