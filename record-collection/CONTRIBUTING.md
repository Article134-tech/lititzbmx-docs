# Contributing to the Record Collection

## Before adding a record

1. Identify the collection or series.
2. Determine the dossier type from what happened in the recording.
3. Assign a stable lower-case record ID.
4. Preserve the original URL, title, description, title card, and raw transcript when available.
5. Separate public material from restricted originals.
6. Document missing material and contradictions rather than guessing.

## Transcript work

Do not silently correct the raw transcript. Add corrections only in a separate working or verified layer. Mark uncertain words and preserve speaker voice.

## Evidence and testimony

Keep speaker recollection distinct from independently verified fact. Add sources to research notes without rewriting what the interviewee said.

## File changes

Every meaningful update should be entered in the dossier’s `docs/revision-history.md` and committed with a descriptive Git message.
