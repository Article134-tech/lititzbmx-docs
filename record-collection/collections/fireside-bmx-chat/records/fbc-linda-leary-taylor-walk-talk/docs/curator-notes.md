# Curator Notes

## Classification

On-location memorial walk, family oral history, and place-based remembrance interview.

## Curatorial interpretation

The footsteps, bridge crossing, surrounding sounds, and destination are part of the record’s meaning. This is not a conventional remote interview; it is a place-based act of remembrance recorded in motion.

## Boundary rule

Curator notes explain the archival structure and recording context. They do not replace the interviewee’s words or convert recollection into verified fact.
