# Flooring-Brochure Presentation Notes

The public landing page deliberately presents the derivative collection as a flooring brochure because the recording begins as a real garage-floor consultation and was published as *Floored: A BMX & Garage Story*.

The presentation layer uses six thematic “systems”: Surface Preparation, Heritage Finish, Race-Grade Performance, Commercial-Grade BMX, Life Beyond the Track, and Final Coat. These groups are encoded in `thematic-groups.json`; they do not alter GH-001 through GH-025 or replace the chronological index.

The brochure language is an access and interpretation device. Exact titles, descriptions, transcript evidence, publication dates, URLs, source captures, provenance, and claim qualifications remain plainly available and machine-readable.
