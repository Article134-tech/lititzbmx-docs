# Curator Notes

## Classification

Experiential interview that begins as a garage-floor consultation and evolves into BMX oral history.

## Curatorial interpretation

The garage-floor consultation is not incidental filler. It is the planned framing device that allowed the conversation to move from Greg Hill’s present work into his BMX past. The dossier therefore preserves both subjects as one historical event.

## Boundary rule

Curator notes explain the archival structure and recording context. They do not replace the interviewee’s words or convert recollection into verified fact.
