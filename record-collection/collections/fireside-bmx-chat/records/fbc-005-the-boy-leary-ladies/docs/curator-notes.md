# Curator Notes

## Classification

Family oral history centered on preserved writings, memorial objects, and multi-generational testimony.

## Curatorial interpretation

The writings and poster board are source objects, not merely props. Their physical survival and recitation create a bridge between private family memory, public memorialization, and the archival recording.

## Boundary rule

Curator notes explain the archival structure and recording context. They do not replace the interviewee’s words or convert recollection into verified fact.
