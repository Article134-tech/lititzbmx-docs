## Record summary

Linda and Kyle discuss Harry Leary’s DIRTWERX era, Livermore Boss frame production, family financial support, a newly acquired NOS DIRTWERX frame, and the provenance of a complete DIRTWERX bike that traveled from Harry to Linda’s son Chaz, back to Harry, through Kyle’s preservation work, and finally back to Linda.

## Why this recording matters

Joins company history to a detailed artifact custody chain and documents the decision to preserve the bike’s wear and history rather than cosmetically restore it.
