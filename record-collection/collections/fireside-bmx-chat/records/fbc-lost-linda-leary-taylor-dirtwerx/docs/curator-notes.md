# Curator Notes

## Classification

Recovered or “lost” conversational record centered on company history, family testimony, artifact provenance, and preservation stewardship.

## Curatorial interpretation

No supporting proposal, outline, press release, clip-note document, or title-card image was submitted with this recovered recording. The absence is documented as part of the record rather than treated as a reason to exclude it.

## Boundary rule

Curator notes explain the archival structure and recording context. They do not replace the interviewee’s words or convert recollection into verified fact.
