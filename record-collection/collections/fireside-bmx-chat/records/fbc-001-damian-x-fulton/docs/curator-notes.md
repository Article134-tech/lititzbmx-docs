# Curator Notes

## Classification

Creative-industry oral history and BMX cultural history interview.

## Curatorial interpretation

The interview intentionally uses a seaside bonfire audio-theater frame. The original recording remains the authority; the scene design and later visual planning are production context, not evidence that all depicted elements were physically present.

## Boundary rule

Curator notes explain the archival structure and recording context. They do not replace the interviewee’s words or convert recollection into verified fact.
