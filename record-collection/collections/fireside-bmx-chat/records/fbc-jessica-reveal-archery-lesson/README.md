## Record summary

Jessica Reveal teaches Kyle archery safety, equipment, and shooting fundamentals while sharing memories of her uncle Harry Leary. The discussion moves through family humor, motorcycles and dirt-bike riding, Harry’s encouragement of Jessica’s archery and hunting ambitions, failure, stubbornness, anger, legacy, and family bonds.

## Why this recording matters
