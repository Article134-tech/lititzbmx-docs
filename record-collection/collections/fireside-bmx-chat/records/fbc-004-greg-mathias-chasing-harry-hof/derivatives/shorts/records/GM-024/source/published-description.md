## Supplied description

In Greg Mathias’ opinion, Harry Leary was one of the most influential people in the sport of BMX. He raced for over 50 years. Greg tells a story of how when he and Harry Leary first started training, Harry would go to Greg’s business. Greg’s wife works there too and didn’t know who Harry was so she asked Harry how long he has been doing this - “Oh about 55 years.” - Also, Greg reflects on younger people not knowing who Harry was but eventually we came to the decision that Harry is still relevant and will remain relevant in the sport of BMX.
