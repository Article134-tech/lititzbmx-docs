## Supplied description

Greg and Kyle find the donation Kyle made to the National BMX Hall of Fame - The 1986 Porsche Design Fallnational plaque as won by Harry Leary. Kyle discusses being inspired by Greg’s donation and worked with Gork who was very helpful with information regarding the plaque. Kyle bought the plaque on eBay and when he realized what he had, he realized he had something bigger than himself as a collector.
