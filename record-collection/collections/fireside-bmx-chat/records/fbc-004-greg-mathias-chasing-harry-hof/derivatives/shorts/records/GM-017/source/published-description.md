**Working-source title:** Fireside BMX Chat w/ Greg Mathias - Harry Leary devalued many of his personal gains

## Supplied description

Harry Leary devalued many of his personal gains because. The riders were told to give the “schwag” away to promote the brand. He ended up keeping very little and holding very little to value. He had a locker in Lake Havasu that was sold off because he wasn’t paying for the unit. Greg never had Harry sign anything, even though he had given him things over the years - he thought he would be around forever.
