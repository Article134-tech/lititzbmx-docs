## Numbering reconstruction

The Studio evidence visibly omits 10 and includes two different records numbered 12. The 32 frames are mapped in chronological capture order to GM-001 through GM-032, excluding GM-010 and inserting GM-012A/GM-012B. This reconstruction is explicit in the frame manifest and every child record.

## Transformation rules

- Original source files are copied without content alteration.
- Stable GM identifiers and descriptive filenames are access-layer metadata only.
- Source wording, spelling, punctuation, and claims are preserved in the source PDF and transcript.
- Descriptive records qualify sensitive claims and distinguish supplied text from archival normalization.
- Missing URLs, video IDs, descriptions, and transcripts are marked missing rather than invented.
