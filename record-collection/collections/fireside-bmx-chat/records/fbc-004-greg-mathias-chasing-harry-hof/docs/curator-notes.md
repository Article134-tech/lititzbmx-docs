# Curator Notes

## Classification

On-location interview and collection encounter conducted inside the National BMX Hall of Fame.

## Curatorial interpretation

The museum objects and the conversation are inseparable in this record. The interview is classified as both oral history and collection encounter because the displayed bicycles and artifacts actively prompt provenance testimony.

## Boundary rule

Curator notes explain the archival structure and recording context. They do not replace the interviewee’s words or convert recollection into verified fact.
