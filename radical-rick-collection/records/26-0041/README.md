## The story

This 35-Year Anniversary Supercross BMX print was created by Radical Rick artist Damian Fulton to celebrate the legacy of Supercross BMX, founded by Bill Ryan. The print is signed by Damian Fulton, connecting BMX racing history with the artwork and visual culture that helped shape the sport. Pieces like this highlight the collaboration between BMX innovators and artists who documented and celebrated the sport’s evolution.
