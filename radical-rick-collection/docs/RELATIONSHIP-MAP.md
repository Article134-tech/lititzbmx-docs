# Relationship Map

## Creator and collection spine

- **Damian X. Fulton** connects original artwork, limited editions, the Radical Rick character, the 35-year Supercross print, the anniversary bobblehead, the Hooligan figure packaging, the Fireside BMX Chat interview and the #RebuildRadicalRick campaign.
- **Lititz BMX** connects the physical holdings, collection records, unboxing dossiers, campaign archive and public Google Sites source page.
- **#RebuildRadicalRick** documents the process and public story of one reconstructed figure; this collection documents the broader body of objects and relationships.

## Cross-collection relationships

- **26.0037** belongs in both The Radical Rick Collection and Harry’s Room. Its canonical ID and ownership are singular; multiple collection membership reflects multiple stories.
- **26.0057** connects Radical Rick artwork with Bill Allen and the RAD film-history records.
- **26.0132** connects Damian Fulton’s character, Hooligan BMX’s custom figure work and John Stancliff / Gap Hill Speed Shop’s scale bicycle and scene.
- **26.0041** and **26.0058** connect Radical Rick with Supercross BMX and Bill Ryan.

## Continuing record trail

The collection points to three compiled unboxing dossiers and the Damian Fulton Fireside BMX Chat dossier. These are not duplicate artifact records; they preserve arrival, examination, testimony and publication context.
