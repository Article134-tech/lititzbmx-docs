# Series and Volume Plan

## Permanent series

`collections/radical-rick-collection/` is the stable collection root.

## Volume One

`volumes/volume-1/` preserves the first published graphic-novel exhibition, *From Page to Artifact*.

## Growth rules

- Corrections, improved photographs and provenance updates may revise existing records through normal versioning.
- Newly accessioned artifacts can be added to the master register without automatically rewriting Volume One.
- A coherent later group may become `volumes/volume-2/`.
- Volume One remains citable as the first published selection even after the collection grows.
